Pinnacle


Pinnacle app helps you keep track of expenses and also calculates how much you have to give or receive to distribute the expenses equally in a group.


Choose Avatar

Enter your name and select the avatar which suits your personality in the best way. Select your most used currency. Your details will be saved in a local device level database.

Home Screen

This page will show you the total amount spent by you on all your logged trips. This page also has the new trip button if you want to log a new trip.
